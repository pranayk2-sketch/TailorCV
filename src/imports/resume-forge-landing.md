Redesign the current ResumeForge landing page to reduce clutter and simplify the product flow while keeping the retro poster-style aesthetic.

The current layout has too many panels and repeated features. The new design should be **cleaner, easier to scan, and visible entirely in one screen**.

Keep the colorful retro blocks, rounded cards, bold typography, and playful stickers, but **reduce the number of sections and move detailed functionality into secondary screens.**

The landing page should only contain **4 main panels**.

---

MAIN DESIGN GOAL

The landing page should act like a **simple control panel** for the app, not a full dashboard.

Each panel is large, colorful, and clickable.

Clicking a panel navigates to a **dedicated page for that feature**.

Remove unnecessary text and internal widgets.
